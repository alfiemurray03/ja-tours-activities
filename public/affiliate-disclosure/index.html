Affiliate disclosure

Clear information about how selected third-party links may support the platform.

JA Group Services Ltd may receive a commission where a customer clicks selected links and completes a booking with a third-party provider.

What this means

An affiliate commission may be paid by the external provider without necessarily changing the price paid by the customer. The presence of an affiliate link does not make JA Group Services Ltd the provider, operator, booking agent or contracting party for the relevant travel product.

Current referral content

The Activities and Destinations pages currently include selected GetYourGuide widgets using JA Group Services Ltd’s affiliate partner ID. Additional partners may be introduced only after review.

Provider responsibility

Any booking is made directly with the relevant third-party provider. Customers should review that provider’s terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules, amendment rules, customer support and complaints process before completing a transaction.
