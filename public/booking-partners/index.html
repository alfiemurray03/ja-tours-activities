Third-party partner options

Selected GetYourGuide activity and city widgets are currently included. Additional partner links and supplier details will be reviewed before wider public launch.

Current GetYourGuide widgets

The Activities and Destinations pages include selected GetYourGuide widgets. These help customers move from discovery and planning to GetYourGuide’s own booking service.

GetYourGuide or the relevant external provider, not JA Group Services Ltd, is responsible for the booking. Its terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules, amendment rules, customer support and complaints process apply.

Before using a provider

  • Check the provider identity and product description.
  • Review the total price, currency and payment terms.
  • Read cancellation and amendment rules.
  • Confirm accessibility and suitability requirements.
  • Keep the provider’s booking confirmation and support details.

JA’s role

JA Group Services Ltd provides discovery and referral content and may provide planning, research and information support. It does not become the operator or supplier merely because a provider link or widget appears on this website.
