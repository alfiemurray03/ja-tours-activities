How it will work

From your initial request to a practical planning pack in three clear steps.

01

Choose your service

Select the planning pack that best matches the support you need and tell us about your trip.

02

Confirm scope and payment

JA Group Services Ltd reviews your request and confirms the scope, final price and estimated delivery time.

03

Receive your PDF

Your researched planning pack is prepared and delivered by email, with one minor revision included.

Secure payment at launch

When services open, secure Stripe payment will follow approval of the customer’s request, scope and final price. Payment controls are not active during this pre-launch stage.

Third-party experiences

Selected activity and experience links may be introduced from approved third-party providers. Customers will book and pay the relevant provider directly under that provider’s terms.

Important pre-launch notice: JA Travel & Experiences is a trading name of JA Group Services Ltd and is not a separate legal entity. JA Group Services Ltd provides travel planning, research and information support. We do not operate flights, accommodation, tours, transfers, insurance or transport services unless expressly stated. Where customers choose to book with a third-party provider, the booking is made directly with that provider and is subject to the provider’s own terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules and customer support arrangements.
